## What happens now

- **Join the Discord** with the button above — that's your line into the builds,
  the roadmap, and the decisions before they're public.
- **Your name on the board.** The [supporters list](support) only shows people who
  opt in. Tell us in the Discord whether you'd like your name shown or kept
  anonymous — either is fine.
- **Manage anytime.** Stripe emailed you a receipt; you can update or cancel your
  monthly contribution from there whenever you like.

Thank you, genuinely — support offered in good faith, **not a product purchase or
general guarantee.** We'll do our best to make it worth it.
